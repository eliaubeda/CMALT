CMALT Portfolio

Aurèlia Puigdomènech - September 2023

Welcome

Welcome to my CMALT portfolio in application for CMALT membership.

At the time of submission I have recently joined the Eden Digital team at the London School of Economics as a Learning Technologist. The portfolio was written while I was working at King's College London.

How to read this portfolio

This portfolio contains a page for each section of the portfolio (see Contents ).

Where applicable, I have highlighted what CMALT core principle it refers to. For example, if something refers to the ‘Communicate & Disseminate’ principle there will be following note: (C&D).

Each principle is shortened as follows:

  • E&U: A commitment to exploring and understanding the interplay between technology and learning.
  • KUTD: A commitment to keep up to date with new technologies.
  • LfC: An empathy with and willingness to learn from colleagues from different backgrounds and specialist options.
  • C&D: A commitment to communicate and disseminate effective practice
Areas of text that show reflection have also been highlighted in this box style.
